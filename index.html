About Hanan Shaah

Hi, I’m Hanan Shaah, a playback singer from Kerala with a deep passion for music and storytelling through melody. Singing has always been my way of expressing emotions and connecting with people on a deeper level. Over the years, I’ve had the opportunity to lend my voice to various film songs and independent projects, each helping me grow as an artist. I love experimenting with different styles — from classical-inspired tunes to modern compositions — and bringing life to every lyric I sing. Music, for me, isn’t just a profession; it’s my journey, my peace, and my purpose.
